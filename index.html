⚠ Safety Information

The Pranayama breathing technique causes physiological changes including lightheadedness and altered states of consciousness. Please read before practicing.

  • Never practice near or in water (bath, pool, sea) — loss of consciousness can cause drowning.
  • Never practice while driving or operating machinery.
  • Always practice lying or sitting in a safe environment.
  • Consult a doctor if you have epilepsy, cardiovascular conditions, or are pregnant.
  • Do not force the breath hold — release voluntarily when the urge arises.
  • Tingling and lightheadedness are normal; fainting is possible.
